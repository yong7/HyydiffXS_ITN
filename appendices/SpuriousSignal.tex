\section{Spurious Signal Fits}
\label{app:spurious_signal}
In this section, the results of the spurious signal fits are shown for each fiducial region and bin of the fiducial differential cross sections. The results are presented as $\frac{S}{S_{\textrm{ref}}}$, which represents the size of the spurious signal uncertainty. The result is shown for all tested functions.
