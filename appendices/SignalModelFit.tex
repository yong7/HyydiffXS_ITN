\section{Signal Modelling Fits}
\label{app:signal_fit}
In this section the signal model fits to the SM Higgs signal simulation,
for each fiducial region and bin of the fiducial differential cross sections,
are shown.
The diphoton invariant mass distributions are fitted with a double-sided
Crystal ball function.
